Speaking Tasks Based on the Video

Task 1: Discussion Questions

Discuss the following questions with a partner or record yourself speaking about each one:

  • What was the main message of the video, and why do you think it's important?
  • Can you relate to any of the experiences or challenges mentioned in the video? Share a personal story if possible.
  • How would you apply the video’s ideas to your own life?

Task 2: Summarize and Reflect

Summarize the video in your own words, then answer the following questions aloud:

  • What is the most surprising or impactful idea presented in the video?

Task 3: Role-Play Conversation
